What is EconoMeme?

EconoMeme is a meme analysis and prediction app. New memes are created by users ever day, and it is up to you to guess if you think that meme will become more or less popular. Use your Meme Coins to bet on memes that you think will increase in popularity, and earn coins when you bet correctly. Additionally, earn Clout by winning big to be featured on the leaderboard!
